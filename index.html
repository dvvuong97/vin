Our strategic collaborators

On 3 April, 2018, Vingroup Corporation officially signed a collaboration contract with two leading universities in the world: Cornell University and University of Pennsylvania in the United States of America. The collaborations reflect the long-term commitment of Vingroup to create a truly world-class university in Vietnam that will be a breakthrough in higher education in Vietnam.
